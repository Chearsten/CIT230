Site Plan CIT230

Site Description

Site Name

The Webb Designs

Site Purpose

The purpose of my site is to show case my personal design talents, give basic and detailed estimates on hourly or packages. My services would include logo/branding, graphics (posters, book covers, labels, promotional material, etc.), website design and development, social media, Google Analytics/AdWords. As well as offer tutorials for small business to set up and run their own sites and social media.

Target Audience

  • Small Business, Hiring Managers
  • Artists (music, literary, etc.)
  • Ages 28-50, male or female
  • Secondary Education, professional, or trades
  • Economic status may vary from medium to high
  • English speaking
  • Possess technical knowledge to access internet and websites on computers, tablets, cells on multiple browsers
  • Wanting to develop a web presence or correlate all platforms on the web
  • Employers wanting to hire a professional to handle their web presence
  • Artists looking for graphic art or web design to show case their work

Personas

Danielle Young

Danielle is a 36 year old Author and runs a culinary school. She is caucasion married and 3 boys ranging from 8-15 years old. She is on several social media platforms and loves working on her laptop but uses her cell for social media and email.

She is finishing her sequel to her last book and is looking for a new graphic artist to do the work on her book cover. She has also thought she should develop a brand for her cooking classes and wants to look at a portfolio before committing.


Oz and Tasha Melgarejo

Oz is a 40 year old small business owner of a event playgym just recently. He is hispanic and married with three small children. He is on several social media sites but realizes nothing matches and confuses customers. He has an outdated website that has been adjusted several times and he continues to break links. While he uses his cell for almost everything his wife has been trying to do things on the main computer for the website and social media. She is interested in running a blog.

He has been trying to run the business and handle the website and social media and is overwhelmed. He would like to hand of the website and social media to someone that can correlate it all. While still letting his wife post things to a blog.
