Welcome to The Mountain Bicycling Shop

Where we have been catering to the cycling community in Bozeman, Montana since 1976! Whether it be road racing, touring, mountain biking, BMX, unicycling, or your first ever bike purchase, we aim to assist you in all of your biking needs. To see our full line of bicycles and accessories, please visit our Brands page. We run a full shop that can do everything from repairing a flat tire, all the way to giving your bike a complete overhaul. With a knowledgeable staff who are all passionate about cycling, The Mountain offers a combined 40 years experience shared between the manager and the owner alone. Additionally, the assistant manager will offer another 14 years of troubleshooting ability! We repair all makes and models when parts are available. Free estimates are always offered upon request. Since 1998, all new bikes sold in this shop have been adjusted and tuned free of charge; however, all additional parts required are extra.

Start an Adventure

Come on in to the shop and pick up a free copy of the Bozeman city bike map. Inquire about our downhill race team, BMX sponsorships, shop rides with groups like the Bandoleros and FOO, factory demo opportunities, and more! The Mountain offers some of the lowest prices in town on repairs and all the latest accessories and toys for all experience levels.
